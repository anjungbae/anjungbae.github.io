Hello and welcome! I am a PhD candidate in political science at the Ohio State University, studying American Politics and Political Methodology. My research interests center on legislative communication in and around congress, with the lenses of causal inference, network science, and experiments.

I am affiliated with IDEA and have a BA in Sociology from Yonsei University in South Korea. I enjoy wildlife and coffee.
